# Class E-File, a data organizer web application

Frontend : Angular
<br>
Backend/Database : Firebase
<br>

<details>
  <summary>Pages Features</summary>

### Connect

- Register by completing information with form control
- Login with failure animation for bad credentials
- Logout is available on menu once logged in

### Categories

- Browse all your categories at a glance
- Create, edit or delete categories to organize your library your way
- Manage genres within each category to refine your classification (for example: “Action” in Movies or “Rock” in Music)

### Stats

- Visualize your personal statistics through interactive graphs
- Track your activity and discover insights about your habits and preferences over time

### Profil

- Modify your profile
- Delete your account (needs confirmation)

</details>
